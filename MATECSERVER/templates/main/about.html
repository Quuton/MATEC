About Us

Bagna is a barangay in the city of Malolos, in the province of Bulacan. Its population as determined by the 2020 Census was 4,947. This represented 1.89% of the total population of Malolos. The household population of Bagna in the 2015 Census was 5,321 broken down into 1,165 households or an average of 4.57 members per household. The adjacent Barangays nearby are Barangay Atlag and Barangay Panasahan. Barangay Bagna has a size of 667,401 square meters and is approximately three (3) kilometers from the town of Malolos. As an urban and coastal barangay, the barangay is known for its main product - the oyster (talaba).
